Training Programme For Students

PURPOSE

Biotech park offers training modules to the UG & PG students doing their course in the field of biotechnology, life sciences, agriculture and pharmaceuticals.
Academic requirements
Industrial skill development
Entrepreneurship development in the field of biotechnology.

"Registration fee of Rs. 500/- to be paid through NEFT; Biotech Park is exempted from GST as per Govt. of India Order/Notification No. 12/2017 dated 28th June 2017."

  • Extraction of essential oil by hydro-distilation method.
  • Quality analysis of soil / water / essential oils etc.
  • Quantificaton of Drugs, Pharmaceutical herbal products.
